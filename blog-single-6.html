Lifeline's Future

By Marc • 4 mins read
Scroll

What would happen to Lifeline in the future?

Since "Lifeline" is a university project developed within a single semester, there are no plans for future updates, and it will remain in its current state. However, if we were to continue working on this project, we would focus on the following improvements: expanding the storyline, introducing additional game elements such as mini-games, and enhancing the overall game experience through improved graphics and sound effects.

While some of these planned enhancements are self-explanatory, one notable idea involves providing Alex with not only predefined answers and replies but also allowing the player to have unlimited, dynamically generated responses through an AI. In this scenario, the player would engage in a chat with an AI, enabling them to write their own responses. This approach aims to enhance the game experience and potentially deepen the connection between Alex and the player.

Once we consider the game to be publishable, our plan is to make it available for people worldwide through platforms like Steam and others. However, in its current state, we believe the game shouldn't be presented to the public, as there are many aspects that we could improve and make more enjoyable with additional time and effort. Despite this, we had a great time working on this project together. If the opportunity arises for us to collaborate again on another project, we would gladly take it on.
